Modernization of BUET Palashi Market

Capstone Project

Worked as the Team Leader in this final undergraduate project that encompasses and interconnects all the sectors of civil engineering and tests us if we're competent enough to be working as professional civil engineers.

With only cursory help from our respected teachers, we, as a team of 7 (seven) members, have completed the project of modernizing an existing market from scratch. Starting from problem identification and demand analysis with the data collected from the stakeholders through surveys and meetings, we have procured the inception report, analyzed the alternate solutions (reviewed by our teachers) and provided the feasibility report after conducting relevant assessments (SIA, EIA, TIA) and analyses (financial and economic feasiblity analysis). In addition to drafting relevant drawings (layout plans and components), we have also made a 3D model of the modernized market for better visualization. As this was a 4.5 credit project, we presented our final presentation to a panel of judges that included a combination of civil engineers and architects.
